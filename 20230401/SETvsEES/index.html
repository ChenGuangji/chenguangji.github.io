3. 传统演化论的主张

Standard evolutionary theory, SET
“Standard evolutionary theory is gene-centered, and treats as evolutionary processes solely those events that change gene frequencies”

3.1 主要因素

  1. Mutation: introduces new variants at random. Repeated occurrence of the same genetic variants is called mutation pressure.
  2. Natural selection: makes adaptive variants more common through differential survival and reproduction.
  3. Genetic drift: random changes in frequency of genetic variants due to sampling.
  4. Gene flow: variants enter and leave a population via migration, dispersal or mating.

4. 扩展演化综论的主张

Extended Evolutionary Synthesis，EES

“The EES is organism-centred, and recognizes other processes in addition to those that change gene frequencies”

4.1 主要因素

  1. Developmental bias: physical development influences the generation of variation.
  2. Plasticity: environment directly shapes organisms’ traits.
  3. Niche construction: organisms modify environments.
  4. Extra-genetic inheritance: organisms transmit more than genes across generations.

Arrows represent causal influences. Processes shown in red are those emphasized by the EES, but not a more traditional perspective

5. SET与EES的简单比较

SET or MS(Modern Synthesis) EES
1. 生物与环境的关系 自然选择为主导因素 互惠的因果关系
2. 遗传的主体 基因是遗传唯一作用单位 遗传是Inclusive的，不仅仅只有基因
3. 变异的随机性 遗传变异是随机的 表型变异非随机（受环境影响）
4. 变化的形式 渐进式 速率可变，存在快速的演化的变化
5. 理论的中心 以基因为中心，演化通常被定义为基因频率的变化 以生物体为中心，演化被重新定义为不同世代间群体遗传特征的变化
6. 宏观演化 通过自然选择、遗传漂变、突变和基因流动的微观演化过程来解释 包括其他的演化过程，包括发育偏差和生态遗传等
