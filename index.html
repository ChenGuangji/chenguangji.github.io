Guangji Chen (PhD. students)
Hi there, I’m Guangji Chen, a PhD. students of University of Chinese Academy of Sciences.
And I am from ZhangLab & FengLab, and participating in the Bird 10,000 Genome Project(B10K) via BGI-Shenzhen. Now, I’m here at Center for Evolutionary & Organismal Biology of ZJU, Hangzhou, China.
My research interest are the radiation evolution & speciation of avian species and evolution & development of birds.
Please feel free to contact me via the email(chenguangji38@gmail.com).
